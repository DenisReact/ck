Once you have found the right tree for your project, you will see one to several size options listed that you must choose from before you add it to your cart. What exactly do those sizes (like #3 container) mean?

All our plants are sold by their container size, except when listed as BULK/bareroot. Container sizes range from small tree pot to 200 gallon nursery pot or wooden crate. The container size options are as follows:

CK also offers InstantHedge products so that one can have a mature hedge to create privacy, a noise barrier, or add a formal design border to the landscape. These come in MiniHedge and InstantHedge size options. Heights of MiniHedges can range from 18-24" tall. InstantHedges come in height options of 3-4', 4-5', and 5-6'.
